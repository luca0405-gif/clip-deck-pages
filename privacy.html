Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Clip Deck ("the app") is a personal, single-user tool used by its owner to prepare and schedule video content for their own TikTok account. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

What data is accessed

When the account owner connects their TikTok account, the app requests:

  • Basic profile information (username, avatar) — used only to confirm which account is connected.
  • Permission to upload video files to the account owner's TikTok inbox as drafts (video.upload scope) — used to send prepared clips for the account owner to review and post themselves inside the TikTok app.

How data is stored

All data, including TikTok access and refresh tokens, video files, and scheduling information, is stored locally on the account owner's own device. Nothing is transmitted to or stored on any third-party server operated by the app's developer. The app does not have a backend server; it runs entirely on the account owner's own machine.

Data sharing

No data is shared with any third party. The only external service contacted is TikTok's own API (open.tiktokapis.com), used solely to upload the account owner's own video drafts to their own account.

Data retention and deletion

Since all data is stored locally, the account owner can delete all stored data at any time by deleting the app's local data folder. TikTok access can be revoked at any time from the TikTok app under Settings → Security → Manage app permissions.
